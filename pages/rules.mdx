# Server Rules

**This is the page where are our server rules are noted. Breaking one of our server rules, will lead into a server ban.**

## Article 1 | General Conduct

- **1.1 Respect**: Treat all members with respect and kindness. Harassment, discrimination, hate speech, or any form of disrespectful behavior will not be tolerated.

- **1.2 Language**: Use language that is appropriate for all members. Excessive profanity, vulgar language, or offensive terms are not allowed.
- **1.3 Behavior**: Engage in civil discourse and avoid disruptive behavior. Spamming, trolling, flaming, or any other form of disruptive behavior will result in disciplinary action.
- **1.4 Multiple Accounts**: Each member is allowed one Discord account. Use of multiple accounts for any purpose, including evasion of bans or harassment, is prohibited.
- **1.5 Impersonation**: Do not impersonate other members, staff, or individuals. Misrepresentation of identity or affiliation is strictly forbidden.


## Article 2 | Content Guidelines

- **2.1 Appropriate Content**: Keep all content appropriate for all ages. This includes refraining from posting explicit material, violent content, or anything that could be considered offensive.
- **2.2 Self-Promotion**: Avoid excessive self-promotion or advertising without prior approval from the moderation team. Unauthorized promotion of external products, services, or communities is not allowed.
- **2.3 Content Ownership**: Respect intellectual property rights and only share content that you have the right to distribute. Plagiarism, piracy, or unauthorized sharing of copyrighted material is prohibited.


## Article 3 | Channel Etiquette

- **3.1 Stay On-Topic**: Keep discussions relevant to the channel they're in. If you want to discuss something unrelated, consider moving the conversation to an appropriate channel or using direct messages.
- **3.2 Bot Usage**: Use bots responsibly and in accordance with channel rules. Avoid spamming commands or disrupting conversations with excessive bot activity.
- **3.3 Voice Channels**: Respect others in voice channels by not speaking over or interrupting them. Follow any additional rules or guidelines specific to voice channels.


## Article 4 | Privacy and Security

- **4.1 Personal Information**: Respect the privacy of other members and refrain from sharing personal information without their consent. This includes but is not limited to: real names, addresses, phone numbers, or other sensitive information.
- **4.2 Account Security**: Keep your Discord account secure by using a strong, unique password and enabling two-factor authentication. Do not share your account credentials with anyone.
- **4.3 Reporting Security Issues**: Report any security vulnerabilities or suspicious activity to the moderation team immediately. Do not attempt to exploit or manipulate security flaws for any reason.
